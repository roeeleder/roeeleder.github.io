About

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. My advisor is Raz Kupferman.

I work in Riemannian geometry and geometric analysis. Specifically, I am interested in overdetermined boundary-value problems for geometric objects, such as curvature, and in topological implications these might have.

I wrote a monograph on generalized Hodge theory. I am currently working on nonlinear applications of this theory in several directions.
